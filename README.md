## What Is This?

Subscription Tracker is a subscription management app designed to give users a simpler, clearer view of their recurring financial commitments.

Instead of guessing what is charging next or piecing together services from emails and bank statements, users can manage everything from one calm, organized interface.

The app is built around a straightforward promise:

- know what you are subscribed to
- know how much it costs
- know when it renews
- know where your money is going

---

## Core Capabilities

### Track Everything in One Place

Users can keep a complete view of their active and paused subscriptions in a single app experience.

- save subscriptions from a predefined service list
- create custom subscriptions manually
- view billing details, dates, category, and notes
- pause or delete subscriptions when needed

### Understand Spending Instantly

The app surfaces the metrics that matter most without forcing users through a complicated finance workflow.

- active subscriptions count
- monthly total spending
- yearly spending projection
- category-level cost breakdown

### Stay Ahead of Renewals

Subscription Tracker helps users see what is coming next before charges happen.

- upcoming payments list
- next billing date visibility
- billing day tracking
- sorted views for faster scanning

### Keep Things Organized

The app turns a messy list of subscriptions into something users can actually manage.

- sorting by next payment
- sorting by price
- sorting by name
- category-based structure for better visibility

### Make Changes Without Friction

Users can manage subscriptions through a focused flow instead of a bloated settings-heavy experience.

- add subscriptions
- edit pricing and billing details
- update notes and category
- export saved data
- clear all stored subscriptions

## App Sections

- active subscriptions count

- create fully custom entries

### Stats

## Why This App

Many finance tools are too broad, too noisy, or too heavy for the simple problem of subscription awareness.

Subscription Tracker is built with a different product mindset:

- focused instead of bloated
- visual instead of overwhelming
- practical instead of overengineered
- calm instead of noisy

It is meant for users who want control over recurring spending without turning subscription management into a project.
